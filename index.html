Labs for APMA E2000

Here we’ll post activities (“labs” for lack of a better word) for the recitation sections of APMA E2000: Multivariable Calculus. The point is threefold:

  • To revisit fundamental material from this week’s lectures.
  • To introduce some tooling for processing/visualizing content on a deeper level than can typically be done by hand.
  • To bring some creative elements into the activities.
